Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie receipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced
Instructions


  1. Preheat oven to 375*F(190*C).
  2. In a bowl,mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the Sugar-cinnaman mixture.
